Research program and Publications

Research

I am fascinated by the unique chemistry biological systems use to survive harsh environments. My research uses informatics to unite classical analytical and organic chemistry with emerging high-throughput DNA sequencing technologies to understand the molecular structures, biosynthesis, bioactivities, and physical properties of plant chemicals, polymers, and composites. My goal is to use this approach to develop and apply new knowledge about chemical biology to sustaining and improving human life.
