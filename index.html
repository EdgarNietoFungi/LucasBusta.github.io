Lucas Busta

Analytical and Organic Chemistry, Biochemistry and Molecular Biology

As a graduate student in the lab of Prof. Reinhard Jetter at the The University of British Columbia, I study plant surfaces. Above all, I am interested in the structure, biosynthesis, and properties of phytochemicals. These remarkable compounds exhibit incredible structural and functional diversity and are important to the everyday life of both plants and humans!
