Lucas Busta

Analytical and Organic Chemistry, Biochemistry and Molecular Biology

As a graduate student in the lab of Prof. Reinhard Jetter in the The University of British Columbia dept. of Chemistry, I study the chemical compounds that comprise plant surfaces. However, I am interested in the structure, biosynthesis, and properties of all phytochemicals, the chemical compounds present in plants. These remarkable substances exhibit incredible structural and functional diversity and play important roles in the everyday lives of both plants and humans! To learn more about phytochemicals and why they are so fascinating please visit my phytochemistry blog.
