I am fascinated by the unique chemistry plants use to survive harsh environments. As a postdoc in the Cahoon lab, my research unites classical analytical and organic chemistry with emerging high-throughput DNA sequencing technologies to understand the molecular structures, biosynthesis, and properties of phytochemicals. My goal is to use this approach to develop and apply new knowledge about plant chemistry to sustaining and improving agriculture. You can read more about phytochemicals and the important roles they play in our daily lives and the global economy on my phytochemistry blog.
